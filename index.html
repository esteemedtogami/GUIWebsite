Dog

This is my dog, Beckett! He is a yellow labrador retriever. We got him as a puppy in April of 2008. He's very smart, but it gets him into trouble sometimes. For example, when he's out in the yard by himself, he has a tendency to get his leash wrapped around chairs and other objects, and sits and barks until someone goes outside to untangle him. However, if he's tangled up and you offer him a biscuit, his ears perk up and he untangles himself and will come to the porch on his own, waiting for his reward. As annoying as this can sometimes be, it's pretty funny too.
